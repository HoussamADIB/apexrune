## Email Best Practices:

1. **Personalization**: Always use the lead's first name and reference their company if possible
2. **Timing**: Send initial emails Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 11 AM or 2 PM - 4 PM
3. **Follow-up**: Send follow-up emails 3-5 days after initial contact
4. **Value First**: Focus on solving their problems, not selling your services
5. **Clear CTA**: Make it easy for them to take the next step
6. **Mobile-Friendly**: Keep emails concise and scannable on mobile devices
