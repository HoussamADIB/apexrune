## General Tips for Image Generation:

1. **Style Consistency:** Use "realistic stock photo style" or "professional corporate photography" in all prompts
2. **Lighting:** Prefer "natural lighting" or "natural daylight" for authentic feel
3. **Setting:** Focus on the client's business environment - their industry, workspace, or operations
4. **No Technology Focus:** Avoid showing specific software, dashboards, or technical solutions - focus on the company's business
5. **People:** Professional business attire, diverse representation, confident poses (when people are included)
6. **Industry Context:** Show the actual business - manufacturing facilities, professional services offices, marketing events, medical devices
7. **Color Palette:** Match the gradient backgrounds used in the case study cards:
   - Platform Paralysis: Blue tones (#DBEAFE, #BFDBFE)
   - Revenue Lifecycle: Gray tones (#F3F4F6, #E5E7EB)
   - Account 360: Light blue (#E0F2FE, #BAE6FD)
   - Event Management: Blue tones (#DBEAFE, #BFDBFE)
   - UX/UI Modernization: Light blue (#E0F2FE, #BAE6FD)
